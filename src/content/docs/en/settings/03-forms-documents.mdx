To access Forms & Documents settings, go to **Settings** in the sidebar and select **Forms & Documents**.
This page has three tabs: **Transfer Voucher**, **Invoice**, and **Code** for embedding widgets on your website.

## Transfer Voucher

Transfer vouchers are documents that can be printed or shared with drivers, containing all the details of a booking.

![](../../../../assets/screenshots/voucher.png "A sample transfer voucher")

<Aside type="danger" title="Legal Notice">
    Transfer vouchers may serve as legal agreements between your company and your customers.
    TransferVista is not responsible for ensuring the legal compliance of your vouchers with local authorities.
    Always double-check the validity of all vouchers generated through TransferVista.
</Aside>

### Generating a Voucher

To generate a voucher for a booking, go to **Bookings**, open the booking details page, and click the **Voucher** button. The voucher will be generated using the settings configured on this page.

### Reference

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Show Payment Method** | When enabled, the voucher displays how the customer will pay. |
| **Show Booking Price** | When enabled, the voucher displays the total price of the transfer. |
| **Starting Point** | The headquarters or base location of your transfer company. This can be overwritten when generating vouchers. |
| **Minimum Lease Duration** | The shortest time a transfer can be leased for, in minutes. |
| **Business Information** | Text that appears at the top of the voucher. Use this to display your company name, address, registration number, contact details, or any other relevant business information. |
| **Agreement Terms Text (English)** | The terms and conditions text in English that appears on the voucher. This typically covers liability, cancellation policies, and service terms. |
| **Agreement Terms Text (Localized)** | The same agreement terms translated into your local language. This version is shown alongside or instead of the English version based on customer preference. |
| **Consent Text (English)** | A consent statement in English that customers agree to when signing the voucher. This might include data processing consent or acknowledgment of terms. |
| **Consent Text (Localized)** | The consent text translated into your local language. |
| **Signature Image** | Your company's signature or stamp image that appears on vouchers. Drag and drop an image file or click to browse. Maximum file size: 8MB. |

## Invoice

Invoices are formal billing documents that can be generated for bookings.

### Invoice Settings

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Business Information** | Text that appears at the top of the invoice. Include your company name, address, tax ID, registration number, and any other legally required business details. |

### Signature Image

Upload a signature image to appear on invoices.

- Recommended dimensions: 300 x 100 pixels
- Maximum upload file size: 8MB

## Code (Embedding)

The Code tab provides HTML snippets for embedding TransferVista widgets on your own website.

All forms and widgets automatically use your branding colors set in [Branding settings](/en/settings/04-branding).
:::
