---
title: General Settings
description: Configure business name, subdomain, timezone, language, currency, and support information
---

To access General Settings, go to **Settings** in the sidebar.

:::tip
  At the very minimum, make sure to set a support email address so customers can reach you with
  questions about their bookings.
:::

## Settings Reference

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Business Name** | Your company name as it appears on booking confirmations, vouchers, and customer communications. |
| **Subdomain** | Used in your booking forms (e.g., `your-company.reserve-transfer.com`). Contact support if you need to update it. |
| **Time Zone** | The time zone for your business operations. Affects all date/time displays in the system. Customers also book in this time zone. |
| **Currency** | The currency for all prices and payments. Changing this does not convert existing prices. |
| **Terms and Conditions URL** | Link to your terms page. Customers must accept these when booking. |
| **Privacy Policy URL** | Link to your privacy policy, displayed on the booking form. Customers must accept the privacy policy before booking. |
| **Support Website** | Your company website or support portal URL. |
| **Support Email** | Email address for customer inquiries. Appears in booking confirmations. |
| **Support Phone** | Customer support phone number. Include country code for international customers (e.g., `+30 210 1234567`). |
| **Review Link** | Link to your Google, Trustpilot, or other review page. Included in "booking completed" emails to encourage customer feedback. |

After making changes, click **Save** in the top right corner to apply your updates.
