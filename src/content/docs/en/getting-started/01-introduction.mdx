TransferVista is a complete booking and operations platform built specifically for transfer companies
and tour operators. If you're running private car services, or guided tours, TransferVista gives
you everything you need to manage bookings, assign drivers, and grow your business.

Take TransferVista on the road with you. Available on all platforms - web, iOS, and Android -
so you can manage your transfers from anywhere.

## Who is TransferVista for?

TransferVista is designed for businesses that provide ground transportation services:

- **Transfer companies** Airport shuttles, hotel transfers, and private car services
- **Hospitality businesses** Hotels and resorts offering guest transportation

Whether you're a solo operator or managing a fleet with multiple drivers and partners,
TransferVista scales with your needs.

### Key Features

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Booking Management" icon="list-format">
    Manage transfer and tour bookings from a central dashboard. Track booking status, handle payments,
    and keep everything organized with calendar and list views.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/bookings/01-overview/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Online Booking Form" icon="laptop">
    Give customers a branded booking experience. Embed a customizable booking form on your website,
    and forget about double bookings with real-time availability checks.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/booking-form/01-overview/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vehicles & Pricing" icon="setting">
    Configure your vehicles, define service areas, and set up flexible pricing with price lists,
    distance or route based rates, and extras.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/catalog/01-vehicles/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Availability Control" icon="approve-check-circle">
    Define where and when you operate. Set service zones, business hours, and block out dates when you're unavailable.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/availability/01-overview/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team & Drivers" icon="comment-alt">
    Add team members with role-based permissions. Drivers get their own interface to view assignments and update booking status.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/team/01-members/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Partner Network" icon="random">
    Work with booking partners who earn commission, or assign transfers to external operator partners when you need extra capacity.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/partners/01-overview/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Communications" icon="comment">
    Automated email notifications keep customers, managers, and drivers informed at every stage of the booking lifecycle.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/communications/01-email-templates/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reports & Invoicing" icon="document">
    Generate reports on bookings and revenue. Create invoices for drivers and partners based on completed transfers.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/reports/01-generating-reports/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="external">
    Connect with Stripe for online payments, sync bookings to Google Calendar, and track conversions with Google Analytics and Ads.

    [Learn more](/docs/en/settings/06-integrations/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
