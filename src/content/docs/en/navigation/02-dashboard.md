The **Transfervista Dashboard** is the central place where transfer providers, hotels, and administrators can monitor their business performance at a glance. It provides real-time insights into bookings, revenue, customer activity, and other key operational metrics.

![Dashboard Screenshot](../../../../assets/dashboard.png)

## What You Can See

### **1. Performance Summary**
At the top of the dashboard, you can view essential business indicators:

- **Total Bookings** within the selected date range  
- **Revenue** generated  
- **New Customers** acquired  
- **Percentage changes** compared to the previous period  

These metrics help you instantly understand how your transfer business is performing.

### **2. Date Range Comparison**
The dashboard allows you to compare performance across time periods:

- Select a date range (e.g., *Month to date*)  
- Compare it with another range (e.g., *Previous month*)  

This makes it easy to identify trends, seasonality, and business growth.

### **3. Revenue Chart**
A graphical chart displays revenue per day:

- Visualizes daily earnings  
- Helps identify peak days and low-activity periods  
- Useful for operational planning and resource allocation  

## What You Can Do

### **Monitor Business Health**
Track bookings, customers, and revenue in real time to understand how your business is performing.

### **Identify Trends**
Use the comparison tools and visual charts to spot growth opportunities, peak seasons, and operational bottlenecks.

### **Plan Resources**
By visualizing daily activity, you can better schedule drivers, allocate vehicles, and prepare for busy days.

### **Quickly Access Management Tools**
The dashboard acts as the main entry point to booking management, customer records, invoicing, team management, and system settings.
